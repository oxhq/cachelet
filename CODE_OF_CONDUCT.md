# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Cachelet to be a professional and respectful open-source project.

Contributors and maintainers are expected to participate constructively, assume good intent, and keep technical discussion focused on evidence, reproducible behavior, and project goals.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, sustained disruption, publishing private information, or conduct that makes project participation unsafe or hostile.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate, edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributions that do not meet this standard.

Serious or repeated violations may result in temporary or permanent loss of participation privileges in the project.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainers through the repository owner profile. Include relevant links, screenshots, or context when possible.

Reports will be reviewed by the maintainers and handled with appropriate confidentiality.
